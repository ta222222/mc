About

Maude Churchill is a London-based creative director with over a decade of experience working at the intersection of fashion, music and youth culture. She builds campaigns, brand worlds and cultural moments that feel native to the communities they speak to.

Her practice moves fluidly between art direction, brand strategy and production — translating loose ideas into coherent visual systems, and partnering with photographers, designers, musicians and studios to bring them to life across film, print and live experience.

She is also co-founder of Don't Be A Creep, a music and arts initiative that works closely with festivals and venues to build safer, more inclusive spaces through creative campaigns — evidence of a practice in which cultural relevance and genuine care are never mutually exclusive.

Currently in London, working internationally.
